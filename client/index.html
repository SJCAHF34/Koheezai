Koheez.ai

Clinical decision support and operational management for AHF HIV pharmacy leadership.

Koheez.ai gives Pharmacy Directors, Regional Pharmacy Directors, and the Chief Pharmacy Officer at AIDS Healthcare Foundation (AHF) a single place to run their HIV pharmacies — from daily operational tasks to AI-assisted clinical assessments and accreditation work.

Key product areas

  • Task Manager — Role-based daily, weekly, and monthly tasks for directors, pharmacists, and technicians, with cross-role priority alerts.
  • Treatment Assessor — AI-powered HIV and PrEP regimen review with drug-drug interaction checking, renal/hepatic guidance, and OpenEvidence-backed clinical notes.
  • ACHC Workbook — Accreditation tracking and document vault for ACHC compliance.
  • Patient Assistance Programs — Quick links and workflows for ADAP, manufacturer copay programs, and patient assistance.
  • Store Performance — Regional and national dashboards covering script volume, revenue, retention, and operational KPIs by store.
  • AI Performance Evaluator — Structured AI-assisted reviews of pharmacy and staff performance.
  • Retention Tracker — Patient retention outreach with automated multi-day email and SMS sequences.

Who it's for

Pharmacy Directors (PDs), Regional Pharmacy Directors (RPDs), the Chief Pharmacy Officer (CPO), pharmacists, and pharmacy technicians inside AHF's HIV specialty pharmacy network.
